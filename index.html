Registration for the San Francisco Brainhack 2020 is now open!

The San Francisco Brainhack 2020 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2019 that is organized by Cameron Craddock . The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.

Dates: February 20-21, 2020

Costs: Breakfast and lunch will be provided: $10 / day. Free admission.

Location: San Francisco, CA, USA

Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2019 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
