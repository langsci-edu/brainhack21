The Brainhack Taipei 2021 is an official satellite event of the International Global Brainhack 2021. The goal of the hackathon is to bring together researchers with disparate backgrounds to collaborate on open science projects in neuroimaging.



Dates: December 11, 2021

Costs: Lunch will be provided: $5 / day. Free admission.

Location: Taipei, Taiwan



Participants are encouraged to post project ideas to www.brainhack.org and to submit a report describing their project to the 2021 Brainhack Proceedings that will be published by Gigascience after the event.

Please join the Brainhack Slack project for updated information on the developing Hackathon content and to contribute your own ideas.
